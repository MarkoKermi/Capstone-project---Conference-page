heating, cooling & vetilation summit 2023

We invite you to the fifth edition of the Heating, cooling and ventilation Engineering Forum, HVAC world Skopje 2023, which will take place on 15 May at the Macedonian Philharmonic. The theme of this edition will be Energy efficiency  and is addressed to investors and developers, contractors, mechanical engineers, consultants and project managers, and companies with solutions for residential and commercial projects. HVAC world Skopje Forum will host Mechanical engineers with outstanding projects. Through the architectural presentations and discussions, HVAC world Skopje Forum offers the latest information and knowledge, and the opportunity for professional networking at the highest level.
